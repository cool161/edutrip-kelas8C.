Pelajaran Bahasa Inggris

The Fun Educational Journey at Yogyakarta and Solo

Hi there! Today I'll tell you about my Edutrip adventure. Let’s start: on the 8th of March 2025, there were 85 people from Santa Laurensia heading to Yogyakarta and Solo. At 5:00 PM, we left for Desa Penting Sari. Once we arrived, we met the head of the village and were introduced to our “orang tua asuh” (foster parents), then rested at their house.

The next day, we went to church at Santa Maria and returned to the village for some fun. On the 9th of March, we made tempe and participated in village activities. On the 10th, we went on a jeep ride around Mount Merapi and learned about the volcano. The 11th was our last day in the village; we packed up and went to Grand Mercure to rest.

On the 12th, we visited museums and the Prambanan Temple. Then came the Solo trip! On the 13th of March, we took a 3-hour journey to Solo and visited the Indonesian Institute of the Arts. We learned how to take good photos and play traditional instruments. After lunch, we checked in at Novotel and rested.

On the 14th, we visited Museum Sangiran, then headed to the airport. After checking in and getting our boarding passes, we went through security and waited to board. During the flight, most of us just slept. Once we landed, we grabbed our luggage and went home to rest.

Conclusion:
This Edutrip took time and energy — just like studying for a test. But it was totally worth it! We had fun, learned new things, and experienced beautiful views. Thank you for reading!
